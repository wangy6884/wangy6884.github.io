There is a story to tell about those two series

In my view, Huygen demostrated the wave property of an object. He said that every point in the wave front is a source of the wave. From the Fourier Series's point of view, this means that every coordinate has its share on any axis in the phase.

Newton, on the otherhand, demostrated the particle property of an object. He said that particle is a point. From the Phase Series's point of view, it means that the sum of the coordinats of the phase can be put into one axis.

It looks like each of them tells one case of the function and we are going to find more applcations of those two series.
